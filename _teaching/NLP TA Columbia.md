### Graduate Teaching Assistant: Natural Language Processing
Worked as a Teaching Assistant for "Natural Language Processing" in Columbia's Graduate School for the Quantitative Methods in Social Sciences Program. The course included hands-on learning of topics like: basics of Python, text tokenization, bag-of-words, NLTK, Stemming, lemmatization, Regex, web scraping, Document Similarity, text clustering, Word2Vec, TF-IDF, Speech tagging, Word Embeddings, Topical Extraction, LAtent Dirichlet Allocation, Lexicons, and Sentiment Analysis on text through Machine Learning.
